About Me

I’m Mohammed Razil Rahees — a passionate Full Stack Web Developer from Kerala, India, who loves creating modern, meaningful, and visually stunning digital experiences. My journey into web development began with curiosity about how technology shapes the world, and it quickly grew into a lifelong passion for building things that inspire and connect people.

I specialize in both front-end and back-end development, working with technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, Express, and MongoDB. I also have experience with version control (Git & GitHub), responsive design, and performance optimization. As a freelancer and startup founder, I’ve built everything from sleek landing pages to full-scale web applications — always focusing on clean code, creative UI, and seamless user experiences. Every project I take on is an opportunity to blend design thinking with technical precision.

Beyond coding, I’m constantly exploring new frameworks, AI tools, and design trends to stay ahead in the fast-paced tech world. I’m passionate about solving real-world problems through smart, scalable, and visually engaging web solutions. My goal is simple — to collaborate with innovative brands and individuals to bring bold ideas to life and build digital products that truly make an impact.
